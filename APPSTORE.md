## Description (4000 char max)

```
TripTalk is a comprehensive harm-reduction resource designed to help adults make safer, more informed decisions in jurisdictions where psilocybin services are legally permitted.

As psychedelic-assisted therapy gains recognition from researchers and licensed practitioners worldwide, access to reliable safety information has never been more important. TripTalk fills that gap with curated, evidence-informed content — all in one place.

KEY FEATURES

◆ Strain & Species Catalog
Browse a detailed reference library of psilocybin-containing mushroom species. Each entry includes identification notes, reported experience profiles, and safety considerations — sourced from published research and community knowledge.

◆ Community Experience Reports
Read firsthand accounts from adults who have participated in legal psilocybin experiences. Reports include setting details, preparation steps, and reflections — helping you understand what to expect before visiting a licensed service center.

◆ Licensed Service Center Directory
Find legally operating psilocybin service centers in jurisdictions like Oregon and Colorado. Listings include licensing status, location, and links to official regulatory databases so you can verify credentials yourself.

◆ Jurisdiction-Aware Legal Information
Stay informed about the legal landscape. TripTalk tracks which states and municipalities have enacted psilocybin regulations, so you always know where services are legally available.

◆ Safety-First Design
Every feature in TripTalk is built around harm reduction. The app includes preparation checklists, integration resources, and links to professional support services — because safety doesn't end when an experience does.

BUILT FOR RESPONSIBLE ADULTS

TripTalk is age-gated to 21+ and designed exclusively as an educational resource. The app does not facilitate sourcing, provide dosing calculators, or encourage any illegal activity. Our community guidelines enforce a strict safety-first standard for all user-contributed content.

We believe that when adults have access to accurate, judgment-free information, they make better decisions. That principle guides everything we build.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

TripTalk does not collect personal data. No accounts. No tracking. No third-party analytics. All content you create — experience reports, reviews, and notes — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

---

Disclaimer: TripTalk is an educational resource and does not provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making decisions about your health. Psilocybin remains a controlled substance in most jurisdictions. This app does not encourage or facilitate any illegal activity.
```

## App Review Notes

```
Thank you for reviewing TripTalk. This is a harm-reduction and educational app for adults, similar in purpose to established platforms like TripSit (tripsit.me) and DanceSafe (dancesafe.org), which provide safety information to reduce harm.

KEY POINTS FOR REVIEW:

1. HARM REDUCTION, NOT PROMOTION — TripTalk provides safety information and educational content. It does not encourage, facilitate, or promote illegal drug use. The app focuses exclusively on jurisdictions where psilocybin services are legally permitted (Oregon, Colorado).

2. NO SOURCING — The app contains no marketplace, no sourcing information, no vendor listings, and no way to purchase controlled substances.

3. NO DOSING CALCULATORS — The app does not include dosing tools or calculators of any kind.

4. AGE GATE — The app implements a 21+ age verification gate at first launch, beyond Apple's 17+ rating requirement.

5. LICENSED FACILITIES ONLY — The service center directory lists only legally licensed, government-regulated psilocybin service centers with verifiable credentials.
```
